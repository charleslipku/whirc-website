## For Faculty & Researchers

Interested in joining the collaborative or exploring an interdisciplinary
project in women's health? We welcome affiliates from every school and college
at the University of Maryland. [Contact us](../contact/) to be added to our
mailing list and directory.

## For Students & Trainees

WHIRC offers mentorship and training opportunities at the intersection of
public health, psychology, bioengineering, and AI — including graduate
assistantships and seed research opportunities. Watch our
[Events](../events/) page for workshops and symposia.

## For Community Partners

Community partnership is one of WHIRC's three pillars. If your organization
works on women's health — from maternal health to health equity — we'd love
to explore how research can support your mission.

## Support WHIRC

To learn about supporting women's health research at the University of
Maryland, please [reach out](../contact/).
